How to Reach Us

Getting Directions

FROM CHOMU TOWN

1
Start from Chomu town centre, take the road towards Morija / Hadota village.
2
Hadota village is approximately 5–8 km from Chomu town along the state road.
3
Ask locally for Madhav Vidyapeeth school upon entering Hadota village.
4
GPS coordinates will be added to this page by the school administration. For now, contact the office for precise directions.

FROM JAIPUR CITY

Take NH-52 (Jaipur–Sikar Highway) towards Chomu. Chomu is approx. 35–40 km from Jaipur city. From Chomu, follow directions above.
