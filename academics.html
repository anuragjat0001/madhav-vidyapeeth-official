How We Teach

Teaching Approach

1
Concept-Driven Learning
Focus on deep understanding over memorisation. Students are taught to apply concepts, not just recall them.
2
Activity-Based Methods
Regular practical activities, demonstrations, and hands-on experiments make learning tangible and memorable.
3
Regular Formative Assessment
Continuous assessments identify learning gaps early, allowing teachers to provide targeted support to each student.
4
Parent–School Alignment
Homework and school diary used to maintain consistent communication and reinforce classroom learning at home.

Academic FAQs

Madhav Vidyapeeth currently operates from Nursery to Class VIII. The school holds CBSE affiliation up to Senior Secondary (Class XII) and will introduce higher classes progressively.
The school uses English as the primary medium of instruction. Hindi is taught as a second language from the Pre-Primary level onwards. Sanskrit is introduced from Class VI.
The school follows NCERT textbooks as prescribed by the CBSE curriculum for all classes. Supplementary reading materials may be recommended by subject teachers.
Primary students (Class I–V) are assessed through Continuous and Comprehensive Evaluation (CCE), which includes periodic tests, oral assessments, project work, and the annual examination.
