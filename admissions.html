Admissions 2026–27

We welcome applications for Nursery to Class VIII. A simple, transparent process — focused on your child, not paperwork.

🎉 Admissions are open for the 2026–27 academic session — Nursery to Class VIII. Contact the school office or fill the enquiry form below.

How to Apply

Admission Process

1
Enquiry
Visit the school office or contact via phone / WhatsApp. Collect the admission form and information brochure.
2
Form Submission
Fill the admission form completely and submit with all required documents to the school office.
3
Document Verification
School staff verify submitted documents within a few working days. Missing documents flagged promptly.
4
Student Interaction
A brief informal readiness interaction for Class I and above. Not a competitive test — simply to understand your child.
5
Fee Payment & Enrollment
Upon confirmation, fees are paid at the office. Student is enrolled, assigned a section, and provided uniform guidelines.
