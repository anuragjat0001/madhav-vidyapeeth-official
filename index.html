About Our School

A School Built for Purpose

Madhav Vidyapeeth is a CBSE-affiliated school established on 1 April 2024 under the aegis of Gramin Shiksha Evam Swasthya Utthan Sansthan, Jaipur. Located in Hadota village, Chomu Tehsil, our school was founded with a clear mission — to bring quality English-medium education to the children of this region.
