Our Story

Founded for a Clear Purpose

Madhav Vidyapeeth was established on 1 April 2024 under the management of Gramin Shiksha Evam Swasthya Utthan Sansthan, Jaipur — a registered society dedicated to education and community well-being.

The school was founded with one clear purpose: to bring quality English-medium, CBSE-aligned education to the children of Hadota and the surrounding villages of Chomu Tehsil — without requiring families to travel to Jaipur city.

Today, under the leadership of Principal Ms. Beenu Yadav (MA, M.Ed), the school currently serves students from Nursery to Class VIII and holds CBSE affiliation (No. 1731221) valid through 31 March 2031, covering the full Senior Secondary level. Higher classes are being introduced progressively.

Vision, Mission & Values

Vision

To provide a nurturing, inclusive environment where every child develops their intellectual, moral, and social potential — becoming a confident, compassionate individual.

🎯

Mission

To deliver quality CBSE-aligned education with integrity, to engage parents as partners, and to build each child's character through discipline, empathy, and a genuine love of learning.

Motto

Every policy and practice at Madhav Vidyapeeth flows from this commitment.
